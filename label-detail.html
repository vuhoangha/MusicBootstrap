LABEL

ARMADA MUSIC


FOUNDED

JUNE 2003

FOUNDER

ARMIN VAN BUUREN - MAYKER PIRON

LOCATION

AMSTERDAM

GENRE

TRANCE . DEEP HOUSE . FUTURE HOUSE . CHILL

DESCRIPTION The story of Armada is one of music, passion and hard work. A story that’s been going strong for more than 12 years. Growing from a boyhood dream into an award-winning company, the Amsterdam-based record label has a very bright silver lining on the horizon. Armada Music was founded in June 2003 by Armin van Buuren, Maykel Piron and David Lewis, whose names abbreviate to ‘ArMaDa.’ Throughout the years, the record company has brought countless quality releases within the wide spectrum of electronic dance music. Outstanding releases in trance, deep house, progressive house, techno and even the pop genre make Armada Music a label that stands out from the crowd. From 2009 to 2013, the Dutch label has been annually awarded the ‘Best Global Dance Label’ title from the International Dance Music Awards in Miami. Working with and managing some of the biggest names in the dance music industry, Armada is home to – amongst others – Aly n Fila, Andrew Rayel, Chicane, Cosmic Gate, Dash Berlin, David Gravell, Gareth Emery, Jochen Miller, John Dahlback, Michael Woods, Orjan Nilsen, Third Party, WnW, and of course, Armin van Buuren himself. Investing in rising talents such as Husman, Pretty Pink, ANOTR, Pelari, Disfunktion, Rodg, and wonder worker Lost Frequencies, Armada Music is able to nurture aspiring artists until they reach the top of their game.
